Ingredients

  • 100g Spaghetti al bronzo
  • 70g Guanciale
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan
  • 50g Pecorino

  • Peper

    • no oil and salt needed. The Guanciale is already salty enought for the whole dish

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles (keep half a cup of the boiling water after the noodles are done)
  2. Keep some of the boiling water after the noodles are done
  3. Cook Guanciale
  4. Place Mix 2 egg yolks with one entire egg
  5. Add Parmesan and Pecorino
  6. Add peper
  7. Mix everyting
  8. Add noddles to the Guanciale
  9. Add Sauce
  10. Mix and enjoy
